Zehra Sura

I am a research scientist at the IBM T.J. Watson Research Lab in Yorktown Heights, New York.

My current work is at the intersection of system optimization and machine learning (ML). I am interested in applying ML techniques to improve systems, as well as building systems to accelerate ML applications.

In the past, I have worked on several accelerator architectures and systems for high performance computing applications including:

  • The Active Memory Cube (AMC), a processor-in-memory architecture based on 3D-DRAM chips
  • NVIDIA GPUs
  • BlueGene/Q
  • IBM Cell Broadband Engine
